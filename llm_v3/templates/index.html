Healthcare PDF Q&A App





Motivation

In healthcare, we're often plagued by loads and loads of documentation and definitions. This is a proof-of-concept app that can take in PDFs and output the relevant healthcare codes and definitions in a compact manner, making the job easier for coders.

Examples

PDF: https://www.amerihealthcaritasnh.com/assets/pdf/provider/resources/HEDIS-coding-guidelines.pdf
